How Fibre is different from DSL?

DSL is dependent on copper wire infrastructure, which is often unreliable and can be slow if you have a bad line or are far away from your local exchange, whereas Fibre transmits data with pulses of light over an optical cable. These cables are far more reliable than copper wires and can offer much greater connectivity speeds.
